Gasoline Challenge

Gasoline Car

If a car has a 40 liter tank. Using gasoline and consuming the entire tank, a journey of 480 kilometers is made. What is the efficiency of the car using gasoline?


here is the code:

let gasoline = 480 / 40;
console.log("The car makes "+ gasoline + " gallons per kilometer");

result: The car makes 12 per kilometer

Alcohol Car

On the other hand, if the car uses alcohol as fuel, the same 40-liter tank covers 300 kilometers. What is the efficiency of the car using alcohol?


here is the code:

let gasoline = 300 / 40;
console.log("The car makes "+ alcohol + " gallons per kilometer");

result: The car makes 7.5 gallons per kilometer
